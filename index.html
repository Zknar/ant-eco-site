Key Features

Swarm Intelligence

We use Ant Colony Optimization and bio-inspired multi-agent systems for autonomous exploration of polluted areas.

Multi-Modal Sensing

Each drone is equipped with modular sensors for gases, water quality, soil analysis, and thermal imaging.

AI-Driven Analysis

Machine learning algorithms detect anomalies, identify pollution sources, and generate real-time environmental maps.

Open Science & FAIR

We commit to open data, open-source tools, and citizen science workshops to ensure accessibility and impact.
